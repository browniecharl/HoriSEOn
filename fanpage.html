Bio

Joe Budden first gained recognition as a rapper with his 2003 single "Pump It Up." Later in his career he was signed to Shady records where he would help form the rap group "Slaughterhouse." He has recently transitioned to podcasting where he hosts the successful "Joe Budden Podcast."
